The Best Movies According to Ryan

My top 3 movies of all time*

*If I can remember any movies I've seen

Inside Out

Literally waterworks every time. Really well done and such a great analogy for our internal landscape as humans.

Mean Girls

The original one, of course. Although I do love the musical, but the movie adaption was a bit trash.

My Neighbor Totoro

This one grew on me gradually. It captures what it's like to be a child and see magic everywhere so perfectly.
